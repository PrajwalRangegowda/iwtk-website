This is disgraceful. They make a show whose name is inspired by a news anchor who just does not shut up, and they expect me to review my experience in less than a hundred words.India Wants to Know is doing to quizzing what Clerk and Ram Gopal Verma Ki Aag did to Hindi films, but in a slightly less classy way. The panellists are sometimes tortured for incorrect answers, but there have been no casualties so far. I would love to be invited back once my wounds heal.

The questions are dead serious and factual and will please any hard core quiz buff but the freewheeling banter and discussions around every question makes it engaging for even a peripheral passerby to get hooked! We get to learn so much more and have a blast doing it! Win win!
Gautam Bhimanni
Cricket Commentator
